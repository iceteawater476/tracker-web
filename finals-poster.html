How to use
01
⏱
Start the Stopwatch
Track how long you've been studying in real time. The timer keeps running even if you leave the app.
  • Hit Start when you sit down to study
  • Hit Pause for breaks — time stops counting
  • When you return, choose to keep, discard or edit the away time
  • Hit Log to save the session to today's record
02
📅
Log Study Time
Missed logging a session? Manually add study time for any past date.
  • Pick any date from the date picker
  • Enter hours and minutes studied
  • Hit Add Entry — it stacks on top of any existing time
  • View your full history with bar charts in the History tab
03
📚
Track Topics
Break each subject down into topics and track your progress through three stages.
  • Add a subject from the sidebar or Subjects tab
  • Add individual topics or chapters inside it
  • Mark each topic as Done Once or Revised
  • Watch your progress ring fill up as you go
04
🎓
Calculate Grades
See your current grade and exactly how many marks you need to reach the next one.
  • Add a subject with your marks obtained and total marks
  • See your current grade and GPA instantly
  • The bar graph shows all grade zones at a glance
  • Update marks anytime to track your progress
05
⚙️
Customise Settings
Make the app yours. Change how it looks and set up your institution's grading rules.
  • Hit the ⚙ button in the top-right corner
  • Choose from 4 themes: Dark, Light, Night, Naval
  • Edit the minimum % for each grade to match your university
  • Default is the Bangladesh UGC grading system
06
📊
Review Your History
See your study patterns over time to stay motivated and spot gaps.
  • View this week's total and all-time hours
  • Bar chart shows every day's study duration
  • Delete any entry with the ✕ button
  • Daily goal is set to 8 hours — the bar fills up

Tips
💡
Works offline. All your data is stored locally in your browser. No account, no sync, no internet needed.
📱
Responsive. Opens as a full app on mobile, and a sidebar layout on desktop — automatically.
⏸
Away time control. If you leave mid-session, the timer pauses and asks what to do with the time when you return.
🎓
Custom grading. Different university? Edit the grade thresholds in Settings to match your institution's system.
⭐
Three topic stages. To Do → Done Once → Revised. Revised topics count higher toward your progress ring.
📈
Retroactive logging. Forgot to log yesterday? Use the manual entry to add hours to any past date.
